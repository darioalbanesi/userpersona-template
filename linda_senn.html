Linda Senn

WHO IS IT / BIO

Linda is 32 years old and works at Bank Mador in Zug. She studied computer science in Zürich and worked at different companies where she used to be a C++ and Java developer. By that time she really missed the interaction with other people and so she decided to move to a more consultant type of job.

At Bank Mador she is responsible to support decision makers on technologies in regard of their website and intranet. She is involved in almost any development on the website and she leads the setup and does also some minor coding when needed (and she really likes to code from time to time!). Her biggest concern is making complex things easy for Users and especially for Content Managers.

Linda knows a lot about technologies and is also a good talker. She can explain things very easy understandable. That is why she also teaches some computer science in her free time.

She is very friendly and nice but you don’t see her laughing a lot. She smiles sometimes and that’s always a very good sign! She lives alone with er dog Bruno, a Jack Russell. She likes to go out in nature and do some hiking or running and takes Bruno always with her.
