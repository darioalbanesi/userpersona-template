Katie Smith

Content Manager

WHO IS IT / BIO

Katie lived in London and moved to Zürich after her studies as a marketing specialist, when she was 26. She is now 31 years old and she is works for a marketing agency in Zürich called Overground. She is the assistant of the CEO and lately also the content manager of their website.

She is very friendly and well educated. She is perfectly organised and she hates it when her plans don’t work out! Sometimes she can even be a bit bitchy about that!

From time to time she gets the task to update some content on the website. Such as new blog postings or new case-studies for their customers as well as adapting the team page with new or leaving colleagues.

Being a content manager is not the favourite part of her job, but sometimes she likes it because she doesn’t have to think too much and she can just type in the content. Most times however, she struggles with the GUI. She forgets where to clock and she can’t find her notes from the initial training she had. Due to the fact that she uses the CMS 1-2 times per month, it always takes time to figure out how to use it.
